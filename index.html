How to play!

The goal of this game is to roll 21 or as close to it as possible without rolling over. You will roll a number, and then choose if you want to risk it and roll again, or hold your number and end your turn. Be careful though! If you roll over 21, you bust and automatically lose that round! Each player will have 3 lives and each round will determined who wins and who loses a life. The winner of the game will be determaned by which player has the last Bob standing! Make sure to press the “next round" button after each round is finished to continue the game. Once the game is finished and a winner is declared, the “new round” button will turn into a “restart game” button. Gamble responsibly and have fun! Try not to kill Bob Barker!
